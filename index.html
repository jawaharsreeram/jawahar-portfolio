ABOUT ME

More than just a developer.

I'm Jawahar Sreeram J S, a Computer Science Engineering graduate from College of Engineering Vadakara.

I began my B.Tech journey in 2022, where I explored different areas of computer science and developed a strong interest in technology, problem-solving, and building meaningful digital solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I worked on projects that helped me understand software development, artificial intelligence, and modern technologies. Beyond coding, I am passionate about digital creativity, photography, content creation, and exploring AI-powered tools. I enjoy combining technical knowledge with creative thinking to build experiences that are functional, innovative, and engaging.

Currently, I am focused on improving my skills in web development, digital technologies, and emerging tools while continuously learning and building projects that reflect my curiosity and creativity.
